Logo process


Initially, I considered incorporating a flower into the logo to symbolize femininity. However, I realized that flowers lacked the boldness and strength I wanted to convey. So, I shifted focus entirely toward reflecting confidence and empowerment.

Pattern & Visual Elements


Logo pattern makes the brand instantly identifiable and unforgettable + it adds a sense of luxury and exclusivity, elevating pieces into something bold.


Viusal elements are any edged shape - square, rectangle, lines - this keep the brand presenting its msg properly. Any other visual elements beside those would be related to event/collab with South Keystone

Fonts


I downloaded this badass typeface on my laptop, thinking I'd never use it. But here we are—it nails the exact vibe this brand needs. Plus, it pairs perfectly with the Outfit typeface, which I always pick by default when it comes to choosing modern & geometric style.

Colors


When you stick to black and white, you’re playing with contrast — one of the most powerful design tools. Why? High contrast enhances readability, draws attention to important elements, and works across different mediums (print, digital, etc.) + It ensures the msg is clear without needing flashy colors.
