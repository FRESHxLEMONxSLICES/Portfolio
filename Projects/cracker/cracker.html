Ideation

The idea hit me while I was playing Red Dead Redemption — Yeah, I game a lot! Their font was Chinese Rocks, which caught my eyes (more on that later). But I didn’t want a western shooter aesthetic. I wanted something louder, tougher, and dripping with raw, aggressive energy!


The glyphs were literally inspired by the cracked wood of the houses.


Some call it 'Vintage,' others swear it screams 'Westy' vibes. At the end of the day, it’s all about how it fits your project. If it works, then that’s all that matters.
