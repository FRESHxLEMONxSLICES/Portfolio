About the old logo

It is unique. Yes, but it feels random. The spacing between the letters is inconsistent, and the thin 'S' stands out awkwardly. Everything in the logo seems disjointed and lacks cohesion. To address these issues and bring a stronger sense of unity, we will redesign the logo with a fresh and cohesive approach.

- Failed Iterations

Challenge

The old logo consistently outperformed my iterations. All of them felt repetitive and failed to offer any uniqueness or improvement over the original design. So, I took a step back, took a break, and dove deeper into research.

What did "more research" achieved?

After doing some research, I found that their social media, perfectly captures the brand's vibe. It’s all about excitement, happiness, and making people smile. It's where most of their audience recognize MALIK’s brand the most.

It's all about Smiling

From funny reels to an interactive community and excitement - filled vibes, it all comes together to build MALIK’s brand. To reflect that cheerful side of the brand, I added a subtle smile into the 'A' as a nod to its personality.

The Website

Finally, I had a blast re-building their B2C website hero page. The new design is clean, modern, and sleek. I also swapped the old font to 'Inter' since the previous one didn’t perform well at smaller sizes.
